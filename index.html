Problem Statements

“We are looking for solutions on Islamic finance in general
and to promote understanding on Islamic banks products “
The problem has been split into 3 challenges.
Participant can attempt to solve any of the challenges listed below.
1
General Public
How can we promote better awareness and
understanding on Islamic finance product
to the general public.
2
Products & Sales
How can we provide better tools to help
salesperson in order to better explain Islamic
financial product to consumers.
3
Shariah Fraternity
How can we provide tools for Shariah
scholar community to help them issue
a Shariah decision.

FAQ

Who can apply GIFF Hackathon 2022?
GIFF Hackathon 2022 is open to all Malaysian citizen and non- Malaysian above 18 years old with minimum 2 people and maximum 4 people per group, regardless of religion and race.
Can we apply as individual?
Yes. You can join this telegram link to find team members.
How can I register to join the program?
Only the Team Leader needs to do registration via online to fill up the official registration form.
Shortlisted teams will be invited to join.
How long is the Hackathon?
Opening Ceremony: 10AM on 3rd October 2022.
Main Event: From 12PM on 3rd October 2022 to 12PM 4th October 2022
Is the hackathon only open to Muslims?
No. This Hackathon is open to all regardless of religion and race that have an understanding of the Islamic Finance.
How can my team submit our solutions for this hackathon program?
Participants will be given a link during the event to submit their solutions.
Where can we get the problem statement?
You can find the problem statement here.
Will there be food?
Yes. Meals and snacks will be provided free of charge throughout the whole program.
Accommodation and transportation.
This Hackathon is one-day-event at the event hall. Transportation is not provided but the parking is provided and free.
Where can I ask for more information about this program?
For more information, you can email us at giffhackathon@kestrl.io, and our dedicated team will be more than happy to answer all your questions regarding the program.

Have any enquiries?

Email us at giffhackathon@kestrl.io
